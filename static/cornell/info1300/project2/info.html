    Directions & Parking

    • Tompkins Consolidated Area Transit (TCAT) offers public bus transportation from all over the county. One-way fares are $1.50 per adult.
    • Cornell University students can ride without a fare on weekends and after 6pm with their student ID card.
    • Residents and visitors can park in the Seneca, and Cayuga Street garages and walk to anywhere in Downtown Ithaca. Please note the Green Garage is closed for construction.
    • Garage parking is $1.00 per hour in the garages. On-street parking is $1.50 per hour during the week until 6pm.

    Accessibility Information

    We are committed to making the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival an inclusive and accessible event for all attendees. Below is key information about accessibility services, including wheelchair access, service animal policies, and on-site assistance.

    Wheelchair Accessibility & Pathways

    • The festival is wheelchair-friendly, with paved and accessible pathways throughout the venue.
    • Designated accessible viewing areas are available for performances and crowded attractions.
    • Accessible restrooms are located near all major festival zones and marked on the event map.

    Service Animal Policies

    • Only trained service animals are allowed at the festival. Emotional support animals and pets are not permitted due to safety and crowd control regulations.
    • All service animals must be leashed and under control at all times.
    • Water stations for service animals are available at designated hydration areas.

    On-Site Assistance & Special Accommodations

    • Festival staff and volunteers are available to assist attendees with accessibility needs.
    • If you require additional accommodations, please visit the Information Booth located near the main entrance.
    • A quiet/rest area is available for individuals who may need a break from the festival's noise and crowds.
